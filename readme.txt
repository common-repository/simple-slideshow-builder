== Description ==

A simple slideshow builder that uses a short code to easily place onto your website! After installing make sure you change the slideshow settings for it to display properly! <br>

Features<br>
** Up to 10 Images <br>
** Customizable descriptions <br>
** Customizable width <br>
** Background color<br>
** Content for images!<br>
** 2 Different Slideshow Layouts! :D<br>
** and more! :D


== Installation ==

1. Download Simple Slideshow Builder
2. Upload Simple Slideshow Builder into your website plugins folder through ftp
3. Place [simplepic] in your posts and/or pages, or place <?php echo do_shortcode('[simplepic]'); ?> onto your template page.
